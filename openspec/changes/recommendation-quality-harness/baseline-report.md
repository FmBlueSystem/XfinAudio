## Evidence-driven finding (revises the PR2 target)

- Collapse is **not** universal and is **not** primarily caused by `build_recommendation_pool`'s
  compatible/fallback partition: `harmonic_journey`, `same_vibe`, and `same_color` use the same
  pool and reach `fill=1.000` with zero collapse.
- Collapse concentrates in strategies with **energy/BPM range filters** that take the
  `strategy-order` path: `chill` (35/40, fill 0.171), `warmup` (16/40, fill 0.527), and
  `peak_time` (7/40). The range filter shrinks candidates and the adjacent BPM-jump guard then
  drops most survivors.
- Therefore PR2 should target the **range-filter + BPM-jump-guard interaction in
  `playlist_service`** for filtered strategies, not the pool partition.

## Secondary bug observed (out of scope, logged)

Using `DJControls(start_path=...)` can crash `recommend_playlist` with
`ValueError: Unknown start_path` when the BPM-jump guard drops the anchor before sequencing
(`optimizer.py:77`). The harness anchors via `manual_order_paths` to avoid it. Candidate fix for a
future change: exempt terminal/anchor tracks from the BPM-jump drop.
